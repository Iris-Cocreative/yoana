A flat editorial illustration for use as an icon. A simplified unicursal labyrinth — the classic single-path form — occupies most of the canvas, centered. The labyrinth has one entrance at the bottom and one destination: a small filled circle at the very center. The path winds inward from the entrance, turns, doubles back, winds inward again — a single unbroken path that leads inevitably to the center. There are no dead ends, no forks, no maze. Only one path.

The labyrinth takes up roughly 80% of the frame. Its outermost ring nearly touches the edges. The center circle is small and solid, like a seed or a destination.

The linework is clean but slightly hand-drawn — the walls of the labyrinth have minor irregularities, slight organic variation in their curves. They are not CAD-precise. They feel like something drawn with a careful but human hand on paper.

There is a small gap at the bottom of the outermost ring — the entrance. This is the only opening. The rest of the labyrinth is continuous, enclosing.

No figures. No text. No symbols inside the labyrinth. Just the path and the destination.

Background: warm cream (#fff6cf), flat.

Color palette: bay blue (#3e7393) for the labyrinth walls. The center destination circle in a slightly deeper bay (#1f4a64). The entrance gap left as cream. Thin umber (#4a2418) as a subtle outer edge on the outermost ring. Background cream. No other colors.

Style: flat editorial illustration. References medieval labyrinth forms, ceremonial walking practices, and the patient, winding nature of long-term community organizing. The labyrinth represents Yoana's approach to political campaigning: not the sprint of a conventional campaign, but the sustained, values-rooted, single-path walk toward a clear destination — taken together, by many people, over time.

Mood: intentional slowness. Long-term navigation. Many people walking the same winding path. Patience as strategy. A clear center worth reaching.

Avoid: maze (multiple paths, dead ends), flag, banner, megaphone, political symbols, donkey/elephant, stars and stripes, raised fists, circuit board geometry, corporate org chart, clipart political imagery, photorealism, gradients, 3D effects, shadows.
