Common questions

Before you reach out.

How long does it take to hear back?

I read every message and respond within a week. For urgent matters, please note that in your subject line and I'll do my best to respond sooner.

Do you take pro bono cases?

I take on a small number of pro bono and sliding-scale engagements each year, especially for survivors of harm and grassroots organizations. Let me know your situation and we'll talk.

Are you available for speaking engagements?

Yes — keynotes, panels, trainings, and university lectures. Reach out via the press email above with details on the event, audience, and timing.

Can you facilitate a process for my organization?

Often, yes. Restorative justice and conflict resolution processes require all parties to be willing to engage. We'll have an initial conversation to assess fit and design the right process for you.

I'm not sure which offering fits — can I still reach out?

Absolutely. Many situations don't fit neatly into one category. Send a message describing what you're navigating and we'll figure out the right path together.
